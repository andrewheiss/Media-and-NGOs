Moratti: Sneijder to stay though 2014-15
Inter Milan president Massimo Moratti has announced a two-year contract extension for Netherlands playmaker Wesley Sneijder.
AP, Thursday 28 Oct 2010
Print
Send
Tweet
Views: 214

Sneijder

Inter Milan president Massimo Moratti has announced a two-year contract extension for Netherlands playmaker Wesley Sneijder. While the club has not issued a formal statement, the ANSA news agency and Gazzetta dello Sport report that Moratti says Sneijder will remain with Inter through 2014-15. Sneijder joined Inter before last season and was a key component in the Italian club’s run to a historic treble, then carried the Netherlands to the World Cup final. Moratti says Sneijder deserves to win the 2010 FIFA Ballon d’Or world player of the year award.
